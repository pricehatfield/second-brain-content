## Overview
A calculator tool designed to evaluate and compare employment opportunities by analyzing financial impacts and compensation package components.

## Tasks
- Design baseline budget validation mechanism
- Create levers for comparing compensation elements
- Develop method for assessing salary, insurance, daycare assistance
- Include evaluation for stock options and retirement matching
- Build flexibility for qualitative factors like work-life balance

## Notes
Initial concept focuses on quantitative and qualitative assessment of employment opportunities with emphasis on family financial considerations.
